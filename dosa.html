DOSA MAKING

Ingredients:

For Dosa Batter:

  • 1 cup parboiled rice
  • 1 cup regular rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds methi seed
  • Water (for soaking and grinding),Salt to taste

For Making Dosa:

  1. Dosa batter
  2. Water (for steaming)
  3. Oil or ghee (for greasing the idli molds)

Making Dosa:

            Heat a non-stick or cast-iron skillet (tawa) on medium heat.
            Once the skillet is hot, lower the heat to medium-low. Grease it lightly with oil or ghee.
            Pour a ladleful of dosa batter in the center of the skillet and spread it in a circular motion to form a thin, even layer.
            Drizzle a little oil or ghee around the edges of the dosa.
            Cook until the dosa turns golden brown and crispy on the bottom.
            Fold the dosa in half or roll it and serve hot with coconut chutney, sambar, or any other accompaniment of your choice.
            Repeat the process for making more dosas. Adjust the heat as needed to prevent burning. Enjoy your homemade dosas!
