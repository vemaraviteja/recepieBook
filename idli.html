IDLI MAKING

Ingredients:

For Idli Batter:

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups idli rice (parboiled rice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds (methi seeds)
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)
  • Salt to taste

For Making Idlis:

  1. Idli batter
  2. Water (for steaming)
  3. Oil or ghee (for greasing the idli molds)

Making Idlis:

            Grease the idli molds with oil or ghee.
            Pour the idli batter into the molds, filling each mold about 3/4 full.
            Steam the idlis in a steamer for 10-12 minutes until they are cooked through.
            Check the doneness by inserting a toothpick; it should come out clean.
            Allow the idlis to cool for a few minutes before removing them from the molds.
            Serve the idlis hot with sambar, coconut chutney, or any other accompaniment of your choice.
